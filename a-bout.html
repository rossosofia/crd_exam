Where is the ball?

Actually, roller derby doesn't have a ball. The whole game revolves around skaters racing around a track. Players earn points by skillfully passing opponents during the action-packed bouts.

THE BASICS OF ROLLER DERBY

Roller derby involves two teams skating around a track. Each team has one jammer aiming to pass opponents to score points, while blockers work to stop the opposing jammer. A match consist of two two 30-minute periods, split into shorter segments called jams, each lasting a maximum of 2 minutes.

Click to discover different roles on track!

JAMMER

The point scorer of the team. They aim to pass through opposing blockers scoring points for each opponent they legally pass. Jammers must be agile and swift to get through the pack.
